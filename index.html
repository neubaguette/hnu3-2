GameDev Process

1

Conceptualization

  • Generate game ideas and concepts.
  • Define the game's genre, setting, characters, story, and gameplay mechanics.
  • Create concept documents or prototypes to pitch ideas.

2

Pre-Production

  • Flesh out the game concept further.
  • Write a detailed game design document (GDD).
  • Create concept art, storyboards, and technical requirements.

3

Prototyping

  • Develop a basic version of the game to test gameplay mechanics and controls.
  • Iterate quickly to identify potential issues early in development.

4

Production

  • Program the game.
  • Create art assets such as 3D models, textures, and animations.
  • Design levels.
  • Develop sound effects and music.
  • Write dialogues and narratives.
